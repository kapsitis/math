Izdalīt ar atlikumu:  
**(A)** $1996$ ar $11$,  
**(B)** $200$ ar $10$,  
**(C)** $15$ ar $1$,  
**(D)** $0$ ar $5$,  
**(E)** $-17$ ar $3$,  
**(F)** $3 ar $12$,  
**(G)** $-3$ ar $12$,  
**(H)** $-18$ ar $3$,  
**(I)** $-111$ ar $7$. 

## Atrisinājums

**(A)** $1996=11 \times 181+5$; $181$ ir nepilnais dalījums, $5$ ir atlikums.
**(B)** $200=10 \times 20+0$;
**(C)** $15=1 \times 15+0$;
**(D)** $0=5 \times 0+0$;
**(E)** $-17=3 \times(-6)+1$;
**(F)** $3=12 \times 0+3$;
**(G)** $-3=12 \times(-1)+9$;
**(H)** $-18=3 \times(-6)+0$;
**(I)** $-111=7 \times(-16)+1$.
