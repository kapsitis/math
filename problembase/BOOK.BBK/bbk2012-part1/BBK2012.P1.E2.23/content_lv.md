# <lo-exercise/> BBK2012.P1.E2.23

Dots, ka $11 \mid (3x + 7y)$ un $11 \mid (2x + 5y)$. Pierādiet, ka 
$121 \mid (x^2 + y^2)$.


## Atrisinājums

$11 \mid x$, jo $x = 5 (3x + 7y) - 7 (2x + 5y)$ un   
$11 \mid y$, jo $y = 3 (2x + 5y) - 2 (3x + 7y)$.   
Tātad $11^2 \mid x^2$, $11^2  \mid y^2$, un $121 \mid x^2 + 3y^2$.
